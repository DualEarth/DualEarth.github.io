PhD Opportunities (Apply by January 15th 2025)

We are currently seeking well-rounded physical scientists with a strong interest in modeling and simulations of Earth's systems, including, but not limited to, hydrology. This PhD is in the Department of Geological Sciences at the University of Alabama. Candidates do not nessessarily need to have a background in Geology, but they should have some experience in an area related to Earth Science demonstrated through project experience or upper division course work. Candidates should have:

  • Background in Earth sciences, physics, Civil Engineering or related fields
  • Strong computational skills with some experience modeling dynamic systems
  • Programming experience and an interest to develop these skills
  • Science communication skills

If you are interested in pursuing a PhD with us, please reach out with an email experessing interest in pursuing a PhD. Please describe your background including your educational background, research goals, and any relevant experience. Please send the email to jmframe at ua dot edu with the subject line "Perspective PhD student".

Post-Doctoral Scholar Opportunity

We have an opening for a post-doctoral scholar to join our team. The ideal candidate will be a physical scientist with expertise in Earth system modeling and simulations. This position is expected to be on-site at the University of Alabama in Tuscaloosa. Remote work may be available for an outstanding candidate with demonstrated experinece collaborating effectively. Required qualifications include:

  • Background in Earth sciences, physics, Civil Engineering, Computer Science or related fields
  • Strong computational skills with expertise in both numerical methods and machine learning for modeling dynamic systems
  • Programming expertise with Python, Matlab, or C++
  • Science communication skills

Please contact us with an email to jmframe at ua dot edu including a letter of interest describing your educational background, technical expertise, research interests and vision, and how you can contribute to our team.
